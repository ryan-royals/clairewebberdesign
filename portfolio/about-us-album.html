About

The goal of this project was to create a special edition 12” album package using experimental image-making processes. For GFlip’s first album ‘About Us’ I use ice to create typography, photographing the ice melting with colour lights to create different effects. The melting ice really reflects the ups and downs of the passionate relationship that is the focus of the album.
