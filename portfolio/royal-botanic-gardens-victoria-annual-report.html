About

The goal of this project was to propose and then design a memorable annual report publication for the Royal Botanic Gardens Victoria. The annual report focused on education, environment, horticulture and science. My proposal was to create a vibrant and scientific looking document and I took macro photos of native flowers using coloured lights to create a sci-fi look.
