The Last Shiraz

Don WebOme
2023
Illustration Packaging design

About

The Monash Don WebOme trio produced one last vintage, The Last Shiraz, from a block of vines that had been grubbed out and burned. The client brief was simply a photograph of the resulting bonfire. I created a modern illustration based on the fire and horizon line in the dim dusk light from the photograph. As the vintage was to be for personal collections, not for commercial use, some fun was requested with the usual warnings and text that is usually required for commercial labels.
